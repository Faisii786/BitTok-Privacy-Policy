# Privacy Policy for BitTok Mesh

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

## Introduction

BitTok Mesh ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our offline peer-to-peer messaging and file sharing application.

## Data Collection

**BitTok Mesh does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

### What We Don't Collect:
- No account information (app requires no sign-up)
- No personal identifiers
- No location data sent to servers
- No usage analytics sent to external services
- No advertising identifiers
- No device information sent to external servers

## Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

- **Messages**: Stored locally in your device's database
- **Media Files**: Saved to your device's storage
- **Device Information**: Used only for local device identification

## Offline Operation

BitTok Mesh operates completely offline:
- All communication happens directly between nearby devices
- No internet connection required
- No data is sent to any external servers
- No cloud storage or backup services

## Permissions

BitTok Mesh requires the following permissions for core functionality:

- **Location**: Required for WiFi Direct and Bluetooth discovery (Android requirement)
- **Bluetooth**: Required for device discovery and connection
- **WiFi**: Required for WiFi Direct peer-to-peer communication
- **Storage**: Required to save received files and media
- **Camera/Microphone**: Required for capturing photos, videos, and voice notes (optional)

**All permissions are used only for local functionality and are never transmitted externally.**

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share your data with:**
- Third-party services
- Advertising networks
- Analytics providers
- Cloud services
- Any external servers

## Data Security

- All communication between devices is encrypted using Android's built-in encryption
- Files are stored securely on your device
- No data leaves your device except during direct peer-to-peer transfers

## Children's Privacy

BitTok Mesh is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data externally, you have full control:
- Delete the app to remove all local data
- Manage permissions through your device settings
- All data is stored locally and can be cleared through app settings

**Summary**: BitTok Mesh is a completely offline application. All data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any information to external servers.
